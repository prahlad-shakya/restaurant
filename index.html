Welcome to Vintage

Serving Hearty Meals & Warm Memories for Over 40 Years.

At The Hearth & Oak, we bring back the golden age of dining — where every dish is made from scratch, every guest is family, and time slows down over a warm plate. Rooted in tradition and seasoned with stories, our kitchen crafts timeless classics using locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients. From comforting stews to hand-rolled pastas and fresh-baked pies, each meal is a tribute to the flavors of yesteryear. Come for the food, stay for the charm. This is where tradition lives on.
